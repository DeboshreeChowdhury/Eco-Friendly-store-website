Fashion for the Future

This week, I am extremely excited about a new sustainable fashion technique called sustainable dyeing. In sustainable dyeing, natural plant-based dyes are used instead of harmful chemicals. In his book, Fashion and Sustainability, John Smith describes it as an eco-friendly revolution in the fashion industry.

Tips for a Sustainable Wardrobe

Looking to make your wardrobe more eco-friendly? Here are some tips:

  • Invest in quality pieces that will last.
  • Opt for timeless styles over fast fashion trends.
  • Accessorize with sustainable jewelry and bags.
