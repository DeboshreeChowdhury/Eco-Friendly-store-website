Environmental Impact of Materials

This table compares the environmental impact of commonly used materials in the fashion industry. Learn how sustainable materials can make a difference.

Material Water Usage (Liters/kg) Energy Usage (kWh/kg) CO2 Emissions (kg/kg) Biodegradability
Organic Cotton 5,000 2.5 1.8 High
Bamboo Fiber 3,000 1.2 1.5 High
Recycled Polyester 1,000 1.0 2.1 Low
Conventional Cotton 10,000 3.0 3.8 Medium
Hemp 2,000 0.8 1.2 High
Leather 15,000 5.0 6.0 Low

Key Takeaways

  • Organic materials like organic cotton and hemp are highly biodegradable and use less water compared to conventional cotton.
  • Recycled materials like polyester have lower water usage but emit more CO2.
  • Conventional materials like leather and conventional cotton have a higher environmental footprint.
